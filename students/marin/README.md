

## Ethics
Ethics is about understanding what is right and wrong, and making choices that consider how our actions affect others. It helps us decide how to act responsibly in harmful situations.

## Intelligence
Intelligence is the ability to understand information, learn from experience, and apply that past knowledge to solve problems. It involves thinking, reasoning, and making decisions based on what we know.

## Harm
Harm is damage or negative consequences that occur when our actions hurt or negatively affect people. Understanding harm is important because it helps us avoid making decisions that cause pain or loss to others.

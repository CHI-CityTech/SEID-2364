# SEID 2364 – Proposed Class Schedule

**Spring 2026 – Modul University Vienna**

This schedule outlines a 14-week progression integrating multimodal delivery (face-to-face, synchronous, asynchronous, and embedded social-media environments), ethical inquiry, and experiential learning using the **Balanced Blended Space (BBS)** framework.

## Detailed Weekly Breakdown

### **Weeks 1–2: Introduction and Foundations**

**Modality:** Face-to-Face (Vienna Residency)  
**Theme:** *What is ethics? What is societal impact?*

**Activities:**
- Course overview and expectations
- Team formation and cohort building
- Interactive BBS mapping workshop (individual → dyad → team)
- Orientation to course platforms (GitHub, Discord/Mastodon)
- Reflective essay prompt: "Personal Ethical Compass"

**Deliverables:**
- Personal Ethical Compass Essay
- Team charter (collaborative norms)

---

### **Week 3: Communication, Mediation, and Agency**

**Modality:** Synchronous Online  
**Theme:** *How mediation shapes ethical meaning*

**Activities:**
- Live seminar with backchannel discussion
- Exercise: Map a digital communication pathway (Source–Vector–Destination)
- Discussion: How does medium affect message and responsibility?

**Deliverables:**
- BBS Mediation Map (individual)
- Discussion contribution

---

### **Week 4: Theories of Intelligence and Moral Systems**

**Modality:** Asynchronous + AI Reflection Logs  
**Theme:** *From Aristotle to Turing to today*

**Readings:** Historical and philosophical perspectives on intelligence and morality

**Activities:**
- AI dialogue log: "What would an ethical intelligence value?"
- Reflective discussion post connecting intelligence theories to moral structures
- Optional: Create multimedia reflection (podcast, video)

**Deliverables:**
- Reflection Post
- AI Dialogue Log

---

### **Week 5: Legal Contexts and the EU AI Act (Part I)**

**Modality:** Synchronous Online (Panel/Seminar)  
**Theme:** *Law as ethical mediation*

**Topics:** Data protection, privacy, algorithmic transparency, GDPR vs. AI

**Activities:**
- Expert or guest panel presentation
- Group case discussion: GDPR and personalization trade-offs
- Q&A and peer dialogue

**Deliverables:**
- Group Case Brief

---

### **Week 6: Cultural Heritage and Ownership Ethics**

**Modality:** Asynchronous Wiki Collaboration  
**Theme:** *Public domain, intellectual property, stewardship*

**Activities:**
- Collaborative case building: "Who owns culture?"
- Class wiki contribution on IP and cultural stewardship
- Research and synthesis of example cases

**Deliverables:**
- Wiki Contribution
- Notes toward personal ethical framework

---

### **Week 7: Legal Contexts and the EU AI Act (Part II)**

**Modality:** Synchronous Online  
**Theme:** *Regulatory simulation and negotiation*

**Activities:**
- Role-play exercise: Policy advisors revise an AI Act section
- Midterm check-in on projects and ethical development
- Reflection on negotiation process as ethical practice

**Deliverables:**
- Policy Revision Proposal
- Midterm progress report

---

### **Weeks 8–9: Ethical Ambiguity and Wicked Problems**

**Modality:** Asynchronous + Hybrid Discussion  
**Theme:** *When there is no single right answer*

**Case Study:** *Quantum Musico: Meta-Mozart Ethics Experiment*

**Activities:**
- Deep-dive case analysis using BBS framework
- Individual reflection on responsibility diffusion
- Small group discussions on authorship and cultural stewardship
- Begin Ethical Logbook entries

**Deliverables:**
- Ethical Logbook entries (multiple reflections)
- BBS responsibility map

---

### **Week 10: Human–AI Collaboration and Agency**

**Modality:** Synchronous Online  
**Theme:** *Designing responsible AI collaborators*

**Activities:**
- Teams design or prompt-tune an AI teammate
- Workshop on ethical coordination and attribution
- Debate: "AI as tool vs. partner"
- Prepare for embedded social-media lab (consent, norms, privacy)

**Deliverables:**
- AI Collaboration Report
- Team protocol for embedded environment

---

### **Week 11: Social-Media Ethics and Virtual Locality**

**Modality:** Hybrid + Embedded Social-Media Environment  
**Theme:** *Ethics of representation and mediation in social spaces*

**Case Study:** *Ethical Distance and Virtual Locality*

**Activities:**
- Participate in private Discord/Mastodon lab
- Observe adjacent public platforms (Reddit, Mastodon, X)
- Map ethical distance across virtual localities
- Reflect on how platform architecture affects moral responsibility

**Deliverables:**
- BBS Ethical-Distance Map
- Reflective essay on empathy and algorithmic influence
- Group report: Cross-platform ethical dynamics

---

### **Week 12: Team Project Development**

**Modality:** Face-to-Face/Hybrid (Vienna)  
**Theme:** *Designing ethical frameworks in practice*

**Activities:**
- Form teams around ethical domains (social media, algorithmic bias, human–AI collaboration, cultural representation)
- Select and extend a case framework (e.g., Quantum Musico, Social Media Locality)
- Develop team proposal and initial BBS mediation diagram
- Coordinate across distributed communication channels

**Deliverables:**
- Team Proposal Document
- Preliminary BBS Mediation Diagram
- Ethics Journal Entries

---

### **Week 13: Iteration, Integration, and Implementation**

**Modality:** Face-to-Face Studio Sessions (Vienna)  
**Theme:** *Refining mediated ethics frameworks*

**Activities:**
- Prototype testing and peer review
- AI integration (optional, with full documentation)
- Synthesis presentations
- Iterative refinement based on feedback

**Deliverables:**
- Updated BBS Diagrams
- Synthesis Presentation (oral or recorded)
- Peer evaluation feedback

---

### **Week 14: Final Reflection and Assessment**

**Modality:** Face-to-Face (Vienna)  
**Theme:** *Synthesis and implications for data science practice*

**Activities:**
- Final project presentations
- Reflective portfolio submission
- Course debrief: lessons, ethical growth, future challenges
- Peer feedback and celebration

**Deliverables:**
- Final BBS Maps
- Reflective Portfolio
- Individual & Group Self-Assessment

---

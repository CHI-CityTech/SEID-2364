## Statement of Collaborative Development

This document discloses direct collaboration with **AI systems** in the design and development of SEID 2364 course materials at Modul University Vienna.

Dr. David B. Smith worked with **ChatGPT (GPT-5)** and other AI tools throughout the project, contributing to:

AI systems served as:

1. **Collaborative Partners** – generating drafts, proposing structures, refining language

3. **Documentation Tools** – organizing materials and ensuring consistency

**Every idea, document, and final decision was reviewed, edited, and contextualized by Dr. Smith.** The process was dialogic—each exchange shaped the next, blending human critical reasoning with AI-based synthesis.

### Additional Tools Referenced

These systems supported integration but did not participate in conceptual authorship.
